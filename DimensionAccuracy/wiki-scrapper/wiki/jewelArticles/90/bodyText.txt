Batang Kali is a town in Hulu Selangor District, Selangor, Malaysia.
The town is a transit point to Genting Highlands, where the casino and theme park is located. The new township of Bandar Baru Batang Kali (Ligamas) is located in this area. The Batang Kali massacre occurred in 1948 during the Malayan Emergency period. Taman Seri is also located at Batang Kali. Taman Seri is oldest place in Batang Kali.
