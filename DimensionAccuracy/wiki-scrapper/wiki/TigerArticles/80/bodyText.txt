California's 53rd congressional district is a congressional district in the U.S. state of California.  It is currently represented by Sara Jacobs, who succeeded Susan Davis following the 2020 election. It is scheduled to be the one California congressional district to be eliminated following the 2020 United States redistricting cycle.
The district is currently in San Diego County. It includes eastern portions of Chula Vista, western portions of El Cajon, central and eastern portions of the city of San Diego, as well as eastern suburbs such as Bonita, La Mesa, Lemon Grove, and Spring Valley in their entirety.
The district will be abolished following the 2020 United States census. It will be the first congressional seat to be lost after a census in California's history.
