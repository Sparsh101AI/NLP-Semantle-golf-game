Law enforcement in Portugal is the responsibility of three bodies:
Guarda Nacional Republicana: National Republican Guard. A gendarmerie who mainly work out of major cities but in 98% of the Portuguese territory, and provides a national highway patrol and fiscal guard as well as a national environment protection police.
Polícia de Segurança Pública: Public Security Police. Civilian police force who work in larger urban areas.
