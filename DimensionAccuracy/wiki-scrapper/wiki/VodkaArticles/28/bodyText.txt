Polity is an academic publisher in the social sciences and humanities. It was established in 1984 and has editorial offices in Cambridge (UK), Oxford (UK), and Boston (US). It specializes in the areas of sociology, media, politics, and social theory. Polity is committed to publishing textbooks and course books for students and scholars.
