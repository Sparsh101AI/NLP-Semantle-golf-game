Saint-Sauveur-sur-Tinée (French pronunciation: ​[sɛ̃ sovœʁ syʁ tine], literally Saint-Sauveur on Tinée; Vivaro-Alpine: Sant Sarvaor) is a commune in the Alpes-Maritimes department in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region in southeastern France.
Its inhabitants are called Blavets or Sansavornins.
